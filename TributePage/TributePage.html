Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

One of the best leaders history has ever seen

  • 1881 - Mustafa was born in Thessaloniki.
  • 1893 - Mustafa starts the Military Preparatory School in Thessaloniki, where her second name is given to her by her teacher, "Kemâl".
  • 1895 - Mustafa Kemal starts the Military High School in Bitola.
  • 1899 - Mustafa Kemal starts the preparatory class of the Military Academy in Istanbul
  • 1902 - Mustafa Kemal graduates from the Military Academy and then attends the Military Academy.
  • 1905 - Mustafa Kemal graduated from the Military Academy as a Staff Captain and was sent to Damascus to serve in the Fifth Army in Damascus.
  • 1906 - Mustafa Kemal and her friends establish a secret association called "Homeland and Freedom" in Damascus.
  • 1907 - Mustafa Kemal is appointed to the Third Army and sent to Salonika.
  • 1911 - Mustafa Kemal is appointed to the General Staff in Istanbul.
  • 1912 - Mustafa Kemal successfully leads the Tobruk offensive in Libya.
  • 1913 - Mustafa Kemal is appointed as Military Attaché to Sofia.
  • 1915 - Mustafa Kemal is appointed as the Anafartalar Group Commander.
  • 1916 - Mustafa Kemal is promoted to Brigadier General.
  • 1918 - Mustafa Kemâl Yıldırım becomes the Group Commander of the Armies
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal sets foot in Samsun.
  • 1920 - Mustafa Kemal opens the Turkish Grand National Assembly in Ankara.
  • 1921 - Mustafa Kemal is appointed as Commander-in-Chief by the Turkish Grand National Assembly.
  • 1922 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal Pasha wins the Battle of Dumlupınar.
  • 1923 - The Republic of Türkiye is declared and Gazi Mustafa Kemal is elected the first President.
  • 1924 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal wears a hat for the first time in Sarayburnu, Istanbul.
  • 1928 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal gives a speech about the new Turkish Alphabet in Sarayburnu.
  • 1931 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal establishes the Turkish Historical Society.
  • 1932 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal establishes the Turkish Language Association.
  • 1934 - The Turkish Grand National Assembly gives the surname "Atatürk" to Gazi Mustafa Kemal.
  • 1938 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal Atatürk passes away.

“Atatürk reminds us of the historical achievements of one of the great men of this century, his inspiring leadership of the Turkish people, his far-sighted understanding of the modern world, and his power and high courage as a military leader. The birth of a free Turkey from a collapsed empire, the honorable declaration of the freedom and independence of the new Turkey, and the preservation of it ever since, are the work of Ataturk's Turkish people. Undoubtedly, there is no example that demonstrates the trust of a mass in him more successfully than the deep and broad reforms he initiated in Turkey.”

-- John F. KENNEDY
